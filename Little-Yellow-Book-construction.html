The Little Yellow Book is a project of the Louisa County Commission on Aging (COA). Its purpose is to provide a directory of contacts for information and services to assist senior citizens in Louisa County and surrounding areas.

The Commission on Aging (COA) is comprised of members approved by the Louisa County Board of Supervisors and is funded by county monies and individual donations. The Commission connects citizens and the Louisa County Board of Supervisors regarding senior issues. The COA conducts and assists other senior organizations with activities and events for the senior citizens of Louisa County, as well as holding monthly meetings which are open to the public.
