    Mission

    Our purpose is to understand and inform the community about aging services and supports that make it possible for older adults to age well at home and in the community. Members try to assess the needs of the aging population in our community and make efforts to provide services to meet those needs.

    About Us

    The Louisa County Commission on Aging (COA) is comprised of members approved by the Louisa County Board of Supervisors and is funded by county monies and individual donations. The Commission connects citizens and the Louisa County Board of Supervisors regarding senior issues. The COA conducts and assists other senior organizations with activities and events for the senior citizens of Louisa County, as well as holding monthly meetings which are open to the public.
